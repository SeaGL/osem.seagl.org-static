SeaGL 2019 - A Prime Year for Free Software

November 15 - 16, 2019 Seattle Central College

Seattle's grassroots free/libre/open source software, hardware, and culture summit returns for the seventh year!

Join speakers and participants from around the world for year seven of Seattle's FREE, as in freedom and coffee, GNU/Linux Conference.

With over 50 talks, four keynotes, and the Cascadia Community Builder Award, this year is sure to be a blast!

November 15th and 16th, 2019
Seattle Central College campus
1701 Broadway, Seattle, WA

Visit https://SeaGL.org for more information.
