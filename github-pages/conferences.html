SeaGL 2019 - A Prime Year for Free Software November 15 - 16, 2019

Seattle/United States

Seattle's grassroots free/libre/open source software, hardware, and culture summit returns for the seventh year!

Join speakers and participants from around the world for year seven of Seattle's FREE, as in freedom and coffee, GNU/Linux Conference.

With over 50 talks, four keynotes, and the Cascadia Community Builder Award, this year is sure to be a blast!

November 15th and 16th, 2019
Seattle Central College campus
1701 Broadway, Seattle, WA

Seattle GNU/Linux Conference 2018 November 08 - 10, 2018

Seattle/United States

SeaGL is a grassroots technical conference dedicated to spreading awareness and knowledge about the GNU/Linux community and free/libre/open-source software/hardware. Our goal for SeaGL is to produce an event which is as enjoyable and informative for those who spend their days maintaining hundreds of servers as it is for a student who has only just started exploring technology options.
