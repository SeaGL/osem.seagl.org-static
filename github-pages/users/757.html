Megan Slater slater

Megan Slater is the Lead Developer in Community Outreach at GenUI in Seattle. She has spent the past 3 years as a web developer working with technologies like React, Ember.js and Node. Megan spent time in an earlier life working in the investment industry and as a classically trained musician. She leverages her experience in multiple fields to encourage great communication and alternate modes of problem solving. Megan also serves on the leadership committee of the Seattle chapter for Write/Speak/Code (WSC). WSC is an organization that is on a mission to increase the visibility and leadership of women and non-binary coders through thought leadership, conference speaking, open source contributions, career development, personal growth and self-care.
